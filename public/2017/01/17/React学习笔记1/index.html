发表于 2017-01-17

React学习捷径：

深入模式: React+ES6+WebPack+React Redux+Fetch+React Router+Immutable+React Native+NodeJs
正常模式: React+ES6+WebPack+React Redux
简易模式: React+ES6+WebPack+React Flux(React官方Flux库)

然后访问 http://localhost:3000 就可以看到初始页面了哦
